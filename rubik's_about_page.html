About Rubik's

Our primary motive is to simplify, encourage, and spread speedcubing competitions as much as possible! Rubik's brings together competition organizers, competitors, volunteers, delegates, and vendors to a single platform where they can efficiently organize, manage, and enjoy speedcubing competitions. We work diligently towards mitigating any delays, miscommunications, cancellations, or any other incidents that might hinder the process of organizing competitions and events.
